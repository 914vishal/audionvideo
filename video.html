POKEMON HORIZONS:THE SERIES

EPISODE 1

At the Glaseado Gym battlefield, Liko prepares for her test, with Grusha's Cetoddle arriving ahead of the Gym Leader himself. Grusha takes his position at the opposite end of the battlefield while Liko chooses Floragato as her first Pokémon. Grusha, in turn, chooses Cetitan as his lead.

EPISODE 2

In "Pokémon Horizons" episode 2, Liko encounters Amethio in a snow-covered forest, leading to a battle where Amethio's Terastallized Ceruledge defeats Liko's Floragato. Spinel's Umbreon, enhanced by Rakurium, interrupts the battle, and Terapagos intervenes, destroying the Rakurium fragment.

EPISODE 3

In "Pokémon Horizons" episode 3, Liko and Amethio find themselves in a cave, while Roy and Dot battle Sango and Onyx, with Spinel's plan unfolding, leading to Amethio's removal from the Explorers and a closer connection between Liko and Amethio.
